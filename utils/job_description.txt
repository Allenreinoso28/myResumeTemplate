Are you a tech-savvy student that thrives in the technical and analytical aspects of server operating systems and configurations? If your answer is yes, apply to the GovTechOn Co-op Program today and allow us to be a part of your first step towards your prospective career path.



GovTechOn is home to professionals who work in partnership with ministries across the Government of Ontario to develop, deploy and maintain technology to deliver enhanced services and business outcomes for Ontarians.



Note: Please confirm that the students who are applying to this position are actively enrolled into the co-op program.



In this role you will:

Maintain CI/CD pipelines for Curam applications, including branching and merging strategies
Troubleshoot build generation and deployment issues to ensure smooth delivery
Participate in operational automation initiatives to improve efficiency and reliability
Software Development:

Write and test code for new software applications.
Debug and troubleshoot existing software.
Participate in code reviews and team meetings.
Data Analysis:

Collect and analyzing data to support business decisions.
Create reports and visualizations to present findings.
Use statistical tools and software to interpret data sets.
Administrative Tasks:

Preparing and maintaining technical documentation.
Creating user manuals and guides for software applications.
Documenting project progress and updates.
Skills required for this role:

Analytical: ability to identify & synthesize information.
Communication: convey information/updates in a clear & concise manner both orally & in written format.
Collaboration: Work with different teams effectively.
Problem-Solving: ability to develop options for resolution / risk mitigation.
Knowledge of Java, JavaScript, VS Code.
Knowledge of GIT, SQL, Selenium, XML and HTML
Knowledge of AI tools is considered an asset.
Knowledge of OOD Data Structures & Algorithms
Knowledge of System Development Life Cycle (SDLC), including planning, requirements, analysis, design, build, testing, implementation, and post implementation activities.
Application Architecture: Understanding of multi-tier application architecture.
Familiar with DevOps Tools: GitLab, Jenkins, OpenSearch, JaCoCo, SonarQube, ZAP
Knowledge of Operating System: Red Hat Enterprise Linux
Knowledge of Database: Oracle
